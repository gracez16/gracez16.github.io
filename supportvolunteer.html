Volunteer and Internship Opportunities

At ABC, volunteers are treasured allies who assist early childhood teachers in their work with infants, toddlers and preschoolers.

ABC's five early childhood programs work alongside youth services staff to establish one-on-one mentoring relationships with school-aged children in ABC's afterschool programs. ABC also opens its doors to families on Saturdays during the school year and on Wednesday evenings during the summer with educational programs for parents and recreational activities for children along with hot meals. Volunteers are especially critical to the success of both.

Get Involved!

Choose one of five volunteering opportunities in our early childhood program.

» Cassidy's Place: Focuses on children from the ages of three to five; who live in poverty and suffer from the debilitating consequences of serious medical conditions and special needs. Located on 419 East 86th street (between First and York Avenue). Program operates year-round, Monday through Friday from 9:00a.m. to 6:00 p.m. through the school year and 9:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. during the summer. For more information about the program and volunteering opportunities please contact Patricia Mckeena at (212)845-3855 or pmckenna@a-b-c.org
